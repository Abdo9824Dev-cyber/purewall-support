Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download wallpapers?

Simply browse categories, tap any wallpaper to view full screen, then tap the download button. The wallpaper will be saved to your Photos app.

How do I set a wallpaper?

After downloading, go to Settings → Wallpaper → Choose a New Wallpaper → Select the downloaded image → Set as Lock Screen, Home Screen, or Both.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free users can access all 14 categories with one quick ad per download. Premium users get exclusive categories, unlimited ad-free downloads, and support independent artists.

Why do I need to watch ads?

Ads help us keep the app free and support the artists who create these amazing wallpapers. Premium users can skip all ads.

Are wallpapers safe to download?

Yes! All wallpapers are curated and optimized for iPhones. We never request unnecessary permissions.

Can I request specific wallpapers?

Absolutely! Email us your suggestions and we'll do our best to add them in future updates.
