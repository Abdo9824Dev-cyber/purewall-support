Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2025

Introduction

PureWall ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our PureWall mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Favorites: Wallpapers you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device
  • Downloads: Wallpaper download history is stored locally on your device

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, wallpaper views, and download statistics (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Technical information if the app crashes to help us improve stability

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the PureWall service
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to add popular wallpaper categories
  • Display trending wallpapers based on community activity
  • Show relevant advertisements (free version only)
  • Provide customer support

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For app analytics and crash reporting (Google)
  • Google Mobile Ads: To display advertisements in the free version

These services may collect information used to identify you. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Wallpaper images are hosted on Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your favorites and download history (via app settings)
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Request deletion of your data

Children's Privacy

PureWall is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@purewall.app
  • Support Page: PureWall Support
